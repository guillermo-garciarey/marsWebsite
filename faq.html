How much does a tattoo cost?

The cost of a tattoo is determined by factors such as size, complexity, and the time required to complete the design. My rate is 100 euros per hour, and I can provide a personalized quote once we discuss the details of your vision.

Is getting a tattoo painful?

Pain tolerance varies from person to person. I utilize modern tattooing techniques and equipment to minimize discomfort. Most clients describe the sensation as manageable. Before your appointment, we can discuss any concerns you may have, and I'll ensure your experience is as comfortable as possible.

How do I choose a tattoo design?

Choosing a design involves a collaborative process. I specialize in creating unique designs that reflect your interests, meaningful symbols, and preferred style. Feel free to browse my portfolio for inspiration, and we can work together to bring your ideas to life.

Are tattoos safe?

Safety is my top priority. I adhere to strict hygiene standards, using sterilized equipment and single-use needles. Additionally, I only use high-quality, hypoallergenic inks. Following my detailed aftercare instructions is crucial for proper healing and minimizing any potential risks.

Can I bring my design or modify an existing one?

Absolutely! Whether you have a specific design in mind or want to modify an existing one, I'm here to help. We can work together to ensure the final design is unique and tailored to your preferences.

How long does it take to heal a tattoo?

Healing times vary based on factors such as size, placement, and your body's natural healing process. Typically, tattoos take 2-4 weeks to heal. I'll provide you with detailed aftercare instructions to promote proper healing, and I'm available to answer any questions you may have during the healing process.

Can I get a tattoo if I have allergies or sensitive skin?

If you have allergies or sensitive skin, it's essential to inform me beforehand. I can recommend suitable inks and take additional precautions to ensure a safe and comfortable tattooing experience.

Can I get a tattoo if I'm pregnant or nursing?

While it's generally advisable to wait until after pregnancy and breastfeeding, we can discuss your specific situation. Your health and safety are my primary concerns, and I'll provide personalized advice based on your unique circumstances.

Do tattoos fade over time?

While some fading is natural over the years, proper aftercare can significantly impact the longevity of your tattoo. I'll provide guidance on aftercare practices, including the use of sunscreen, to help preserve the vibrancy of your tattoo.

How do I find a reputable tattoo artist?

Look no further! Read reviews from satisfied clients, explore my portfolio to see examples of my work, and feel free to ask any questions you may have. My dedication to providing quality work, combined with my passion for the craft, ensures that you'll receive a unique and professionally executed tattoo. Let's work together to turn your tattoo ideas into a stunning reality.
